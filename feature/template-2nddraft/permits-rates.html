Parking Permits

BCIT offers a variety of parking permits for students, faculty, staff, and visitors. Permits allow you to park in designated areas without paying hourly rates.

Student Permit

$75.00 / month

For currently enrolled BCIT students.

  • Valid in all student-designated lots
  • Requires valid student ID
  • One permit per student
  • Monthly or semester billing available

Faculty & Staff Permit

$95.00 / month

For BCIT employees with a valid employee ID.

  • Valid in faculty/staff designated areas
  • Requires valid employee ID
  • Payroll deduction available
  • Reserved spots available (extra fee)

Visitor Permit

$15.00 / day

For guests and visitors to BCIT Downtown Campus.

  • Valid in visitor-designated areas
  • Available at the parking office
  • No advance booking required
  • Valid for one calendar day

Accessible Permit

Free

For individuals with a valid accessible parking placard.

  • Valid provincial placard required
  • Designated accessible stalls only
  • Register with the parking office

How to Get a Permit

Permits can be purchased online through your MyBCIT account or in person at the Parking Office. Follow these steps:

  1. Sign in to your BCIT Parking account
  2. Go to My Reservations → Purchase Permit
  3. Select your permit type and billing period
  4. Enter your vehicle information
  5. Complete payment
  6. Your permit will be emailed to you within 1 business day

EV Charging Stations

BCIT Downtown Campus has electric vehicle charging stations available in the North Parkade and East Parkade. Charging is available on a first-come, first-served basis.

Location Charger Type Number of Stalls Rate
North Parkade – Level 2 Level 2 (240V) 6 stalls $1.50 / hr
North Parkade – DC Fast DC Fast Charge (50kW) 2 stalls $0.35 / kWh
East Parkade – Level 2 Level 2 (240V) 4 stalls $1.50 / hr

EV charging stalls are also subject to regular parking rates. Please ensure your vehicle is actively charging — idle vehicles may be ticketed after the charging session ends.
