Frequently Asked Questions

Sign in to your BCIT Parking account, go to Find Parking, select an available lot, and click Reserve a Spot. Choose your date and time, confirm your vehicle details, and complete payment. You will receive a confirmation email.
Go to My Reservations, find the booking you want to cancel, and click the Cancel button. Cancellations made more than 2 hours before the reservation start time are fully refunded. Cancellations within 2 hours may be subject to a $2.00 fee.
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and debit cards. Cash is accepted at the parking office in person only. The BCIT Parking app also supports Apple Pay and Google Pay.
Parking tickets can be paid online through your account or in person at the Parking Office. If you believe the ticket was issued in error, you can submit a dispute within 14 days of the issue date. Contact us at parking@bcit.ca with your ticket number and a brief explanation.
Yes! If spots are still available, you can extend your reservation through the app or website up to 30 minutes before your current booking ends. Go to My Reservations → Active → Edit to extend your time.
Yes. Accessible parking stalls are available in all BCIT Downtown Campus parking areas. A valid provincial accessible parking placard must be displayed. Please register your placard with the Parking Office to receive your free accessible permit.
Our system uses sensor data from each parking stall to detect occupancy in real time. The map on the Find Parking page updates every 30 seconds. Green means available, yellow means limited (fewer than 5 spots), and red means full.
Click Sign In and then Forgot password?. Enter your registered email address and we will send you a password reset link. The link expires after 24 hours. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact us.
